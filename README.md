## About Research Journal Management System

The Research Journal Management System (RJMS) is an intuitive and efficient platform designed for managing the publication process of academic journals. Our goal is to streamline the workflow for editors, reviewers, and authors, providing a seamless experience in the research publication lifecycle.

### Key Features

- **User-Friendly Submission System**: Simplifies the process for authors to submit their manuscripts.
- **Editorial Workflow Management**: Supports editors in managing submissions, peer reviews, and editorial decisions.
- **Reviewer Assignment**: Facilitates the matching of reviewers based on expertise and availability.
- **Real-Time Notifications**: Keeps users informed about important updates via email and dashboard notifications.
- **Statistical Reporting**: Provides insights into submission statistics, acceptance rates, and reviewer performance.
- **Customizable Submission Forms**: Allows journals to tailor submission forms to fit specific requirements.

RJMS is designed to be flexible, robust, and user-friendly, making it suitable for various academic fields.
